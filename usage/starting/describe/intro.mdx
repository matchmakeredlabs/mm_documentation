# Building Described Collections

The most foundational feature of MatchMaker is it's collections: a unique format enabling matching of knowledge across a variety of sources. In this section, we will discuss:
1. [How to Create a Collection](/usage/starting/describe/create): creating your first collection
2. [How to Build a Collection](/usage/starting/describe/hierarchy): transforming learning assets, competency-based frameworks, and curriculums into MatchMaker's hierarchical format for your new collection
3. [How to Describe Elements](/usage/starting/describe/): representing knowledge in your collections using Palet<sup style={{fontSize: 8}}>TM</sup> keys
